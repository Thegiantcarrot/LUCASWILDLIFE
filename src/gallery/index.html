Lucas Henry Photography

Elephant Footsteps

This photo was captured when I was in Zimbabwe. The editing process to turn it into B&W was very rewarding as the final image is one of my best images to date. Shot with Canon EOS R3.

Lion Relaxing

Deep in Mana Pools I captured this photo just before the sun went down, this gives the image a warm golden vibe. Taken on the Canon EOS 6D MK ii.

The Eye

In the Okavango Delta I spotted this baby crocodile too close to the boat to photograph his whole body with my 600mm lens. so I opted for this menacing photo of his eye. Shot on Canon EOS 6D MK ii

Lion Sunset

The portrait of this lion was taken in Mana Pools in Zimbabawe when I stepped out behind the truck to sit on the ground and get this level shot. Shot on Canon EOS 6D MK ii
